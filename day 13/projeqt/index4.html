basketball

  1. Georgia's national basketball team has seen growing success. Georgia co‑hosted EuroBasket 2021, and made their FIBA World Cup debut in 2023, finishing 16th globally.
  2. Notable Georgian basketball figures include Zaza Pachulia, Tornike Shengelia, and others who played internationally. As of early 2024, Georgia ranked around 23rd in the world in men’s basketball.
