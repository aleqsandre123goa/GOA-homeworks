Football

  1. Football is one of Georgia’s most popular sports. The Georgian Football Federation oversees the national men’s, women’s, and futsal teams.
  2. The national men's team (nicknamed Jvarosnebi, or The Crusaders) made their debut at a major tournament during UEFA Euro 2024, reaching the Round of 16 — a historic achievement.
  3. Willy Sagnol is the current head coach, with Guram Kashia as captain and the country's most-capped player
