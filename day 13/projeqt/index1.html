1. Rugby Union
  2. Rugby is the top team sport in Georgia, driven in part by the traditional game lelo burti, which influenced the national style and nickname
  3. “Lelos.”
  4. The Georgian national rugby team has consistently performed well in the Rugby Europe Championship, winning it nearly every year since 2006 (except 2017), including in 2025.
  5. Domestic rugby features competitions like the Didi 10 and the club Black Lion, which also competes internationally.
